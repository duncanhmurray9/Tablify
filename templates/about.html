Tablify was made out of necessity (and laziness) due to the fact that I cannot decide what songs to learn on guitar. I thought that displaying the guitar tab links from my own playists and from my top songs would help me decide a little bit better. Now I have too many choices!


Tablify does not store any user data, Spotify account logging in is facilitated by the Spotify itself and so no user information is stored by Tablify.
