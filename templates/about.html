About


Tablify was made out of necessity (and laziness) due to the fact that I cannot decide what songs to learn on guitar.

I thought that displaying the guitar tab links from my own playists and from my top songs would help me decide a little bit better. Now I have too many choices!

If you would like to see more of my projects, then please visit my Github: github.com/duncanhmurray9
